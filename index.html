FAQ

Q. Is there a subscription?

The app offers optional premium features. You can use core features without a subscription.

Q. Where is my data stored?

Your progress is stored on your device. If you enable backup/restore, your data may be stored in your cloud account.

Q. I changed devices. How do I restore my data?

Use the app’s backup/restore feature (if enabled) and sign in with the same account.

Q. The app is not working correctly.

Please include your device model, OS version, and a brief description of the issue when contacting support.
